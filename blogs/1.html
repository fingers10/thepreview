Bringing to you The Prodcast Playbook this 2020

Posted By admin | 01 January 2020

Amateurs sit and wait for inspiration, the rest of us get up and go to work.

~Stephan King
And, one fine morning, this quote hit us hard. What could have been possibly passed off as a cozy Sunday turned into a battleground of thoughts. At The Prodcast, we always wanted more—more quality, more productivity, and more capacity. The question of what could help us reach beyond and break the barriers today’s product enthusiasts face kept irking us. And, then, just like a swift breeze, something dawned upon us—what powerful tool than writing could help us connect better with our product folks? Hence birthed the idea of The Product Playbook—our one-stop platform for ideating and curating product-based stories and conversations.

Launching a new product? Exploring product marketing techniques? Starting up? Practising design thinking? Don’t worry, we’ll have you covered with out focused articles that are centered around multiple avenues of the product world. From easy checklists to mini guides and no-brainer reads, our stories are a matter of minutes for boosting your product knowledge. This 2020, as fancy and special as the yea sounds to be, we’re all set to embark on our journey of penning down powerful product blogs and connecting with promising personas, all along.

We wish you a very Happy New Year! Stay tuned to The Product Playbook.

Cheers,
Team
