Product Launch Checklist

Posted By admin | 10 March 2020

Customers, positioning, go-to-market strategy, revenue—are these buzzwords giving you a challenging time? Not anymore! 

Product marketing is an important aspect of launching a product, and getting it right means stitching together different technology and content processes. From creating the befitting positioning statement and website messaging to finding a niche audience and setting up promotions—a product marketer’s schedule is jet-packed with key deliverables.

Huge or small—product launches always need to be orchestrated. To help you with successful launching, we’ve curated some important pointers, broadly revolving around customers, content, and product, in the form of a compact checklist.

Customers:

 ✔ Finding the ideal customer segments 

 ✔ Setting up user feedback mechanism

 ✔ Crafting a solid customer engagement plan

Product:

 ✔ Determining the pricing models and plans 

 ✔ Analyzing the product journey for enhancements

 ✔ Collaborating with partners and resellers

 ✔ Finding the right promotional channels and mediums 

 ✔ User sign-up, onboarding, and adoption

Content:

 ✔ Deciding on the product story and messaging 

 ✔ Finalizing the website structure, layout, and CTAs

 ✔ Creating periodic ads, campaigns, and promotions

 ✔ Working on the sales collaterals  

 ✔ Getting the press release kit (articles and presentations) ready
